# Blood is only expelled from the heart when it contracts.  心脏收缩时,血液才从心脏射出。

常见：vi.开除，驱逐出境

expel sth from sth

## contract  v.感染，收缩减小

supply decrease → supply contract 供给减少

muscle contract 肌肉收缩

常见：n.合同  v.订立合同，约定建立…协议

to contract into sth  订约加入某事

to contract with sb to  与…某人签订合约

under contract / enter into a contract with or to sb / sth 和某人某物签定合约

## exert 施加利用发挥

施加 pressure / influence / force (impose)

exert a force on sth 给某物施加作用力

exert an influence on sth 对…产生影响

利用 exert every effort to do 尽一切力量
